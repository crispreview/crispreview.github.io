It is the 13th of July. England have qualified for the World Cup Semi Finals. It is time to celebrate the only way I know how, by eating a packet of "INSPIRED BY English Bangers and Mash FLAVOURED POTATO CRISPS" and writing a short crisp review rather than replying to emails.

Because it has been 30 degrees for almost a week, sausage and mash opportunities have been at an all time low. I don't want the oven on, and I don't want to stand over a pan of boiling potatoes. It's a shame, because sausage and mash is the king of dinners. 

I don't think I've had mash for at least a month...

Fortunately, these crisps taste just like sausage and mash. Herby, meaty, and greasy. They're delicious. Standard walkers crisps with plenty of flavouring.

10/10

If only they did pea flavoured crisps as an accompaniment.
